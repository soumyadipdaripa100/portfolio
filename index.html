Registration and Login page

This is a blood donation app. Before starting the app either you have to log in or register.


Landing Page

In this page you can see three buttons with three purposes. 'Request for blood' button is for requesting blood to others, there you have to fill a form with patient details. 'Nearby Requests' button is for searching the needy ones. You have to filter with your blood group, district and city, then you can contact with them if you are willing to donate blood. 'Available Donors' button is to see who are available to donate blood and if you need blood urgently, you may contact with them. There you have to filter again with the dropdowns to see people matchig with your blood group.
